PoloChat Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

PoloChat is a free global nearby friends chat app for users age 15 and older. PoloChat uses location privately to rank suggested friends. Normal users do not see another user's age, date of birth, email, phone, city, country, exact location, exact distance, online time, or suggestion reason.

Data We Collect

We collect account information such as username, display name, email, optional avatar, date of birth for age safety, device metadata, report/block safety data, message metadata, active messages, and approximate location metadata used for discovery ranking.

Chat Privacy

PoloChat auto-deletes chats based on your selected setting. Authorized super-admin moderation can review active safety-related chats only with reason and audit logging. PoloChat should not be described as full end-to-end encrypted while this moderation capability exists.

Location Privacy

Location is used internally for suggested friends and safety. Public user APIs do not expose exact coordinates, city, state/region, country, exact distance, distance bucket, or suggestion reason.

Controls

Users can report, block, request a data export, and request account deletion inside the app.

Contact

Support email must be configured before Play Store upload.
