PoloChat Terms of Service

Last updated: May 20, 2026

PoloChat is a free Android and iOS mobile app for nearby friends chat. Users must be age 15 or older.

Safety Rules

Do not harass, threaten, impersonate, exploit, spam, or upload illegal content. Users must send and accept a friend request before private chat or calls are enabled.

Unsupported Features in V11 Alpha

PoloChat V11 Alpha does not include payments, subscriptions, reels, feeds, stories/status, public profile posts, video uploads, ads, or monetization.

Moderation

PoloChat may suspend or delete accounts that violate safety rules. PoloChat auto-deletes chats based on your selected setting. Authorized super-admin moderation can review active safety-related chats only with reason and audit logging.

User Controls

Users can report, block, export data, and request account deletion inside the app.

Contact

Support email must be configured before Play Store upload.
