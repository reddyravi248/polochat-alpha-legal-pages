PoloChat Account Deletion

PoloChat users can request account deletion in the app from Settings or Privacy & Account.

If you cannot access the app, email the PoloChat support address listed in Play Console from the email connected to your account and include your username.

Deletion requests suspend the account first, then permanently delete account data after the configured grace period unless the request is cancelled. PoloChat may retain limited safety, security, abuse-prevention, or legal records when required.
